ü•´ Harvesting Guide

When to Harvest:

  • Green stage: Mature but not fully ripe
  • Color stage: Fully ripe (red, yellow, orange)
  • Use sharp scissors or pruners

Storage Tips:

  • Refrigerate: 1-2 weeks
  • Freeze: 6-8 months
  • Dry: For long-term storage

Expert Tips for Maximum Yield

  • Pinch off first flowers to encourage bushier growth
  • Support plants with stakes or cages when heavy with fruit
  • Harvest regularly to encourage more production
  • Peppers turn sweeter as they ripen on the plant
