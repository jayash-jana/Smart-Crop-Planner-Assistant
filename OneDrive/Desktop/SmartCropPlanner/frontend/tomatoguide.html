‚úÇÔ∏è Pruning Tips

Do:

  • Remove suckers when small
  • Prune lower leaves
  • Remove yellowing leaves
  • Use clean, sharp tools

Don't:

  • Prune in wet weather
  • Remove too many leaves
  • Prune determinate varieties heavily

ü•´ Harvesting Guide

  • Harvest when fully colored but still firm
  • Pick regularly to encourage more production
  • Store at room temperature, not refrigerator
  • Expected harvest: June - July (60-85 days after planting)

Pro Tips for Maximum Yield

  • Plant marigolds nearby to repel pests
  • Use mulch to retain moisture and prevent disease
  • Provide support with cages or stakes
  • Rotate crops yearly to prevent disease buildup
