Frequently Asked Questions

Tomatoes need 1-2 inches of water per week. Water deeply 2-3 times per week rather than light daily watering. This encourages deep root growth.
Peppers respond well to balanced fertilizers (10-10-10). Apply at planting time and again when fruits start to form. Avoid too much nitrogen.
Most vegetables prefer pH 6.0-7.0. You can test using our soil test kit or send samples to your local extension office.
Start tomatoes and peppers 6-8 weeks before last frost date. For spring planting, that's typically late February to early March.
